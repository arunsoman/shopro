# Shopro Restaurant POS: Product Capabilities

Shopro is planned as a VAT-compliant, inventory-centric POS solution designed to bridge the gap between front-of-house service and back-of-house procurement.

## 1. Unified Order Management
Streamline every transaction type through a single, intuitive interface.
 * **Omnichannel Billing:** Support for Dine-in, Takeaway, and Delivery.
 * **Table Intelligence:** Live floor maps with real-time occupancy status.
 * **Flexible Checkout:** Split billing, multi-modal payments, and tiered discount management.
 * **UAE Compliance:** Fully automated 5% VAT invoicing with offline mode and auto-sync capabilities.

## 2. Smart Kitchen Operations
Minimize errors and maximize throughput with digitized routing.
 * **KDS & KOT:** Digital Kitchen Display Systems or traditional thermal printing.
 * **Intelligent Routing:** Automatically route orders to specific stations (e.g., Bar, Grill, Pastry).
 * **Modification Tracking:** Clear visibility on "No Onions" or "Extra Sauce" requests to reduce waste.

## 3. Inventory & Recipe Precision
Control your margins by tracking every gram of ingredient.
 * **Recipe Mapping (BOM):** Link every menu item to specific ingredients for automatic stock depletion.
 * **Wastage Control:** Track variances and spoilage to identify "hidden" costs.
 * **Automated Alerts:** Low-stock notifications based on real-time sales velocity.

## 4. B2B Procurement Integration (The Shopro Edge)
 * **Direct Sourcing:** Access supplier catalogs and place orders directly from the POS dashboard.
 * **Price Benchmarking:** Compare costs across multiple suppliers to ensure the best margins.
 * **Cost Analytics:** Real-time visibility into your Food Cost Percentage.

## 5. Financial Intelligence & Compliance 
 * **Audit-Ready Reporting:** Daily Z-reports, sales reconciliation, and category-wise performance.
 * **Accounting Export:** Seamless data transfer to major accounting software.
 * **Shift Accountability:** Granular cashier logs, shift-close reports, and cash drawer reconciliation.

## 6. Growth & CRM Tools
Turn one-time diners into regulars.
 * **Customer Profiles:** Maintain a database with order history and preferences.
 * **Loyalty Integration:** Points-based rewards and SMS/Email promotional triggers.
 * **Performance Dashboards:** Monitor hourly trends and "Hero vs. Zero" menu item analysis.
